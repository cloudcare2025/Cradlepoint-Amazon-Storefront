Modular Modems

Hot-swappable cellular modems that upgrade router connectivity without replacing the entire device.

Cradlepoint MC400-5GB

5G Sub-6 modular modem for E3000 and E300 series routers. Hot-swappable, field-upgradeable. Enables 5G connectivity on compatible Cradlepoint enterprise routers.

5G Sub-6 GHz Hot-swap capable Compatible with E3000/E300

Cradlepoint MC20BT

LTE Cat 20 modular modem with Bluetooth. For E-series routers requiring LTE fallback or primary LTE connectivity with BLE for IoT sensing.

LTE Cat 20 Bluetooth 5.0 Hot-swap capable

Managed Accessories

Cloud-managed extension devices that add PoE, switching, and connectivity to your router deployment.

Cradlepoint RX20

Compact managed PoE switch accessory. Adds 4 PoE ports to Cradlepoint routers. Cloud-managed through NetCloud for zero-touch provisioning and remote monitoring.

4x GbE PoE ports 60W PoE budget NetCloud managed Compact form factor

Cradlepoint RX30

Multi-port managed accessory with additional Ethernet ports, serial, and GPIO. Extends router capabilities for complex deployments with legacy serial devices and GPIO sensors.

Additional Ethernet ports Serial RS-232 GPIO connectors NetCloud managed

Power Supplies & Adapters

OEM power supplies, PoE injectors, and DC power solutions for all deployment scenarios.

AC Power Adapter (Standard)

Standard AC power adapter for indoor Cradlepoint routers. Multiple regional variants available (US, EU, UK, AU).

12V / 3A AC input 100-240V Indoor use

Vehicle Power Adapter

DC vehicle power adapter for mobile router deployments. Wide voltage input for fleet vehicles, emergency vehicles, and transit.

9-36V DC input Ignition sense Fused

PoE Injector

IEEE 802.3at PoE+ injector for powering Cradlepoint routers over Ethernet. Simplifies installations where AC power is not available at the router location.

802.3at (30W) Single port Indoor rated

Mounting Brackets & Hardware

Professional mounting solutions for wall, ceiling, DIN rail, and vehicle installations.

DIN Rail Mount

Industrial DIN rail mounting bracket for S700, S400, and IoT router series. Standard 35mm DIN rail compatible for industrial enclosures and control panels.

35mm DIN rail Steel construction Tool-free install

Vehicle Mount Kit

Ruggedized vehicle mounting kit for R-series mobile routers. Under-seat, trunk, or dashboard mounting for fleet and first-responder deployments.

Vibration-rated Universal fit Steel/aluminum

Wall/Ceiling Mount

Universal wall and ceiling mounting bracket for E-series enterprise routers and AP2600 access points. Supports multiple orientations for optimal antenna positioning.

Wall or ceiling mount VESA compatible Includes hardware

Cables & Connectivity

Antenna cables, serial cables, GPIO cables, and power extension cables for all deployment needs.

GPIO Cable

GPIO cable for connecting sensors, relays, and external devices to Cradlepoint routers with GPIO support. Enables automated actions based on environmental inputs.

4-pin GPIO 1m length Shielded

Serial Console Cable

RS-232 serial console cable for local router management and legacy serial device connectivity. Essential for out-of-band management and serial passthrough applications.

RS-232 DB9 1.8m length Straight-through

Antenna Extension Cable

Low-loss antenna extension cable for relocating cellular antennas away from the router. Ideal for installations where the router cannot be placed near a window or exterior wall.

SMA connectors Low-loss LMR-195 Available in 3m/5m/10m

Battery Backup

Battery solutions for uninterrupted operation during power events.

Battery Module for R-Series

Integrated battery backup module for R-series mobile routers. Provides runtime during vehicle ignition cycles and brief power interruptions, keeping critical connectivity online.

Li-ion 30-minute runtime Hot-swap capable
