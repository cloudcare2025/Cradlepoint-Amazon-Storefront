About Cradlepoint

Enterprise-Grade 5G

Cradlepoint is the global leader in cloud-delivered 5G and LTE wireless network edge solutions. With purpose-built routers and adapters deployed across more than 500,000 enterprise endpoints worldwide, Cradlepoint enables organizations to replace or augment wired connections with always-on cellular broadband. From branch offices and pop-up locations to mobile fleets and IoT deployments, Cradlepoint delivers the performance and reliability enterprises demand.

Cloud-Managed via NetCloud

Every Cradlepoint device is managed through NetCloud, a unified cloud platform that provides zero-touch deployment, real-time visibility, and advanced SD-WAN and SASE security capabilities. NetCloud enables IT teams to configure, monitor, and troubleshoot thousands of endpoints from a single dashboard, dramatically reducing the time and cost of managing distributed networks. Policy-based automation ensures consistent security posture across every site.

From the Manufacturer

5G Pioneer

Cradlepoint launched the first enterprise-class 5G router and has shipped more 5G wireless WAN endpoints than any other vendor. Our routers are purpose-built for cellular, not retrofitted from wired platforms, delivering superior performance and reliability.

Part of Ericsson

Acquired by Ericsson in 2020, Cradlepoint combines its wireless WAN expertise with Ericsson's global 5G infrastructure leadership. This means deeper carrier integration, faster access to new spectrum capabilities, and a roadmap backed by the company that builds 40% of the world's mobile networks.

NetCloud Platform

NetCloud is the single management plane for every Cradlepoint device. Zero-touch provisioning gets routers online in minutes. Centralized policies enforce security uniformly. Real-time analytics and AI-driven insights help IT teams resolve issues before users notice them.

Global Support

Cradlepoint provides 24/7 global technical support with carrier-certified engineers who understand both the device and the network. Backed by Ericsson's worldwide service organization, customers get rapid resolution and proactive monitoring for mission-critical deployments.
